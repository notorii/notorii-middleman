Back to simplicity, back to purity.1

notorii is a multidisciplinary firm with a singular focus; timeless design.

In a world of increasing visual clutter, thoughtful, clear, design solutions stand out from the noise. We believe in more whitespace, fewer colors, and fewer typefaces.2

We are Product Designers, employing a hollistic methodology. The same quality and craftsmanship is applied to every touchpoint and medium.

Past projects include Branding & Identity, Product Design, Software & Mobile App Development, Photography, Advertising, and Copywriting. The proper design approach can be applied to any medium, and we are open to a variety of creative projects.

We believe in working with clients that state their problems clearly, and put their trust in us as designers to solve them.
